chocolate quinoa cake

your shooping list:

  • 2 cups cooked golden quinoa
  • 11/2 cups water or milk
  • 1/3 cup cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup butter,melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsb baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • parchment paper

steps

  1. whisk the milk,eggs and vanilla.Add the quinoa and mash up.
  2. In another bowl,mix all the dry ingredients.
  3. combine both.
  4. Bring your oven to 350 farenheit.Grease the parchment paper in your cake pan, bake for 40mins.
